Buying or selling?

Find the right yacht — then survey it with confidence.

Through our partnership with The Find Group, we connect clients with a top-tier luxury yacht brokerage for buying or selling — and then provide the independent survey that protects the transaction. Two trusted specialists, one smooth process.

Why this matters

A broker finds the yacht. A surveyor protects you.

These are two different jobs — and they should be done by two different people, with no conflict of interest between them.

The Find Group represents buyers and sellers with deep market knowledge and an exceptional inventory. Once you've found the right vessel (or a buyer for yours), we step in as your fully independent surveyor to verify exactly what you're getting. You benefit from a great brokerage experience and an unbiased, defensible survey — with clean handoffs throughout.

No conflicts

We never accept referral fees that bias our findings, and we never survey a vessel we've brokered. The partnership is about coordination, not influence.

How it works

From listing to launch

Connect with The Find Group

Tell them what you're looking for — or that you're selling. Their team handles the search, the listing, the negotiation, and the paperwork that comes with a sale.

Find the vessel

Shortlist, view, and agree on a target. Your broker will help you make an offer contingent on a satisfactory survey.

Bring us in to survey

We conduct the independent pre-purchase survey — hull, systems, machinery, sea trial, and eco-flag — and deliver a defensible report within 48 hours.

Close and cruise

Use the survey findings to finalize, renegotiate, or walk away. Then take delivery of a vessel you truly understand — and enjoy her.

Tell us what you're after — we'll make the right introductions.
