Our network

Partners & Associations

No surveyor works alone. These are the organizations we trust and collaborate with across brokerage, conservation, and the wider marine industry — each chosen because they share our standards.

TFG

The Find Group

thefindgroup.com
Yacht Sales & Brokerage

A full-service luxury yacht brokerage representing new and brokerage yachts across power and sail. Our long-standing partnership means clients get seamless handoffs between purchase and survey — and access to exclusive listings. The Find Group refers buyers to us for independent pre-purchase surveys, and we refer clients ready to buy or sell to them.

Visit thefindgroup.com
CCY

California Coastal Yachts

californiacoastyachts.com
California Brokerage

A boutique brokerage specializing in California-based vessels — from day boats to long-range cruisers. Their local knowledge of West Coast marinas, yards, and cruising grounds complements our SoCal survey coverage. When a client is buying or selling along the California coast, we work together to make transactions clean and well-informed.

Visit californiacoastyachts.com
EYG

Eco Yacht Group

ecoyachtgroup.com
Sustainable Yachting

Our sister concern in the sustainability space — a collective focused on hybrid and electric propulsion, solar integration, and clean-yacht technology. They're our go-to resource when a client wants to understand or upgrade a vessel's environmental footprint, and our eco-flag findings often feed directly into their refit roadmaps.

Visit ecoyachtgroup.com
OAN

Ocean Alliance Network

oceanalliancenetwork.org
Ocean Conservation

A non-profit alliance uniting marine researchers, conservationists, and ocean-minded businesses. Their science informs the environmental benchmarks we measure vessels against in every eco-flag section — so our findings reflect the latest understanding of what keeps coastal and open water healthy.

Visit oceanalliancenetwork.org
WFB

Wigcraft Flying Boats

wigcraftflyingboats.com
Advanced Marine Tech

Specialists in wing-in-ground (WIG) effect craft and next-generation marine platforms, Wigcraft represents the innovative edge of waterborne transport. Our partnership keeps us at the forefront of emerging vessel types — so when unconventional or experimental craft need surveying, we bring current, informed expertise.

Visit wigcraftflyingboats.com
TRI

Trinacle

trinacle.com
Web Design & Marketing Automation

Our digital partner — the team behind the design, development, and marketing automation for Eco Yacht Surveyors and the wider Find Group of companies. Trinacle builds the websites, CRM integrations, and automated workflows that keep our practice running smoothly behind the scenes, and manages our Twenty CRM instance.

Visit trinacle.com
A note on independence

Our partnerships never compromise our impartiality. We accept no referral fees that would bias our findings, and we never survey a vessel we've brokered. The relationships above exist to connect our clients with trusted specialists — and to keep us at the leading edge of the industry.
