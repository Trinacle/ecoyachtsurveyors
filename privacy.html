Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Eco Yacht Surveyors ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we handle it when you use ecoyachtsurveyors.com or work with us.

Information we collect

When you request a survey or contact us, we collect the details you provide: name, email, phone, and information about your vessel and enquiry. We also collect limited technical data (such as the pages you visit) through standard analytics.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide survey services.
  • To send your information to our CRM (Twenty CRM) so our team can follow up.
  • To communicate about your survey, report, and related services.
  • To improve our website and services.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your data. We share it only with our CRM and tools needed to deliver our services, and where required by law. Survey reports are shared only with parties you authorize (such as your insurer, broker, or yard).

Data retention

We retain enquiry and client records for as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time, subject to our record-keeping obligations.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, email admin@ecoyachtsurveyors.com.
