New Build & Refit Oversight

Commissioning a build or a major refit is one of the largest investments in yachting. Our independent stage inspections protect your specification, your warranty, and your delivery timeline — at every milestone.

Discuss your project (949) 229-1733
Your advocate in the shed

Someone has to hold the yard to your contract.

A shipyard's priority is its build schedule. Your priority is getting exactly the yacht you specified, built to standard, on the timeline you were promised. Those don't always align — and that's where we come in.

We attend the yard at agreed milestones to verify that construction matches the build specification, that materials and workmanship meet class and ABYC standards, and that any deviations are documented and resolved before they're buried under the next layer of fiberglass or teak. The result is a yacht delivered on spec, on time, and with a clean warranty position from day one.

  • Spec verification at each build stage
  • Laminate schedule & material compliance checks
  • Systems commissioning & sea-trial acceptance
  • Eco-spec validation for hybrid/electric builds
