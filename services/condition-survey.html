Yacht Condition Survey

An annual or pre-refit health check that catches small problems while they're still small — and gives you a prioritized maintenance plan that protects your vessel's value.

Book a condition survey (949) 229-1733
Preventive, not reactive

The most expensive repair is the one you didn't plan for.

A weeping stern-tube seal, a battery bank nearing end-of-life, or a hairline gelcoat crack can each become a five-figure emergency if left unchecked. A condition survey finds them first.

Lighter in scope than a pre-purchase survey but just as rigorous where it counts, the condition survey is ideal for owners who want to stay ahead of maintenance, prepare for a refit, or simply know their vessel is sound before a long cruise. Every finding is ranked by urgency with budget guidance, so you can plan your spending instead of reacting to it.

  • Moisture & thermal imaging of hull and deck
  • Systems, machinery, and safety-equipment audit
  • Prioritized maintenance plan with cost guidance
  • Ideal pre-refit baseline document
