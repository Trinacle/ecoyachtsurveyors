Yacht Consulting & Advisory

Not every question needs a full survey. Sometimes you need a seasoned expert in your corner — for a refit plan, a green upgrade, a purchase negotiation, or a delivery acceptance. That's our advisory practice.

Book a consultation (949) 229-1733
Expertise on call

The decisions that define ownership.

The biggest moments in yacht ownership — buying, refitting, upgrading, accepting delivery — are also the most expensive to get wrong. A few hours of expert guidance at the right moment can save tens of thousands.

Our consulting practice draws on decades of experience across hundreds of vessels. We're equally comfortable advising a first-time buyer on a 45-footer and consulting on a hybrid drivetrain retrofit for a 50-meter. And because conservation is core to who we are, every engagement can include an eco-efficiency lens.

How we help

Advisory services

Refit planning

Scope, sequence, and budget guidance for refits — from a systems overhaul to a full rebuild. We help you plan work that adds value, not just cost.

Eco-upgrade roadmaps

Solar, hybrid propulsion, battery systems, watermakers, and waste treatment — phased plans to reduce your vessel's footprint and operating cost.

Buyer's representation

We act as your eyes and advocate through a purchase — shortlisting, viewing, evaluating, and negotiating — so you buy the right yacht at the right price.

Delivery acceptance

Final acceptance inspections at handover — verifying the vessel meets contract before you take delivery and the final payment falls due.
