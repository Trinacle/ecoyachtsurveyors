Where we work

Miami, Newport Beach, and wherever your yacht sits.

We survey across two coasts and beyond. Our primary regions are South Florida and Southern California — and for vessels further afield, we travel. If your vessel is afloat, we can usually get to her.

South Florida

Miami & the surrounding region

South Florida is one of the yacht capitals of the world, and it's a region we know intimately — from the megayacht marinas of Miami Beach to the boatyards of Fort Lauderdale and the cruising grounds of the Keys.

  • Miami & Miami Beach — Miami Beach Marina, Sea Isle, Bayside, Key Biscayne
  • Fort Lauderdale & Broward — Bahia Mar, Pier Sixty-Six, Lauderdale Marine Center
  • The Florida Keys — Key Largo to Key West, Marathon & Stock Island
  • Naples, Tampa & the Gulf Coast — Sarasota, St. Petersburg, Clearwater
  • The Bahamas & Caribbean — a short hop for a Florida-based team

See the Florida marinas we serve

Home waters

Newport Beach & Southern California

Our home port is 670 Lido Park Dr., Newport Beach — the West Coast's premier yachting hub. We have same-week availability throughout SoCal's major marinas and boatyards.

  • Newport Beach — Balboa, Lido Village, Mariners Mile
  • Greater LA — Marina del Rey, Long Beach, San Pedro
  • San Diego — Shelter Island, Sunroad, Chula Vista
  • Santa Barbara northward — Ventura & Channel Islands
  • Mexico — Ensenada & Cabo San Lucas

See the California marinas we serve

Beyond the coasts

Worldwide coverage

For vessels further afield, we travel. Our surveyors regularly attend yachts across North America, the Caribbean, the Mediterranean, and the Pacific — coordinating travel and yard time into a single fixed quote.

  • The U.S. East Coast
  • Antibes, Palma & the Med
  • Pacific Northwest & Canada
  • The Great Lakes
  • Hawaii & the South Pacific
Not on the list?

If your vessel is somewhere unusual, just ask. We've surveyed in shipyards from Turkey to Tasmania — distance is rarely the obstacle people assume.
